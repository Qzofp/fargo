Get all movies + last played
----------------------------
http://localhost:8080/jsonrpc?request={"jsonrpc":"2.0","method":"VideoLibrary.GetMovies","params":{"properties":["lastplayed"]},"id":2}

Get all episodes + last played
------------------------------
http://localhost:8080/jsonrpc?request={"jsonrpc":"2.0","method":"VideoLibrary.GetEpisodes","params":{"properties":["lastplayed"]},"id":3}
http://localhost:8080/jsonrpc?request={"jsonrpc":"2.0","method":"VideoLibrary.GetEpisodes","params":{"properties":["lastplayed"],"limits":{"end":100,"start":0}},"id":7}
